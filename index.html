block-buster.eth

block-buster.eth is a minimal blockchain explorer that connects to your own web3 endpoint such as metamask. When connected to your own full node, you thus have a way to visualize a state of the Ethereum blockchain without relying on third parties. In such a setting block-buster also protects your privacy by not exposing data that you are querrying to third parties.
